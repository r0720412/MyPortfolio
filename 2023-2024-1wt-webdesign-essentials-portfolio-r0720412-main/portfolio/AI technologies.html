AI technologies

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a core technology in AI that involves training algorithms to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data

Robotics process automation

Definition: RPA involves the use of software robots or 'bots' to automate highly repetitive, routine tasks usually performed by humans.

Deep Learning

Deep Learning, a subset of ML, utilizes neural networks with many layers (hence "deep") to analyze large volumes of data.

Computer vision

Definition: Computer Vision allows machines to interpret and understand visual information from the world, akin to human vision.
